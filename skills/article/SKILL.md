# Article - Blog Post Creation

Write comprehensive articles ready for publishing.

## When to Use

Trigger on:
- "write article about"
- "create blog post"
- "write content for"
- "draft article"
- "long-form content"

## Workflow

1. **Research**: Check for existing research, gather info
2. **Outline**: Create structure with sections
3. **Write**: Full article with SEO optimization
4. **Optimize**: Meta description, keywords, links
5. **Save**: Notion page with full article

## Length Options

- short: ~800 words
- medium: ~1500 words (default)
- long: ~2500 words
