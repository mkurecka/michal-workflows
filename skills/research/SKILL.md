# Research - Topic Research

Comprehensive research on any topic using web search and trend analysis.

## When to Use

Trigger on:
- "research [topic]"
- "find information about"
- "what's trending in"
- "analyze [topic]"
- "gather data on"

## Workflow

1. **Web Search**: Current articles, news, statistics
2. **Competitor Analysis**: Existing content, gaps
3. **Trend Analysis**: What's trending, recent developments
4. **Compile**: Key findings, stats, quotes, angles
5. **Save**: Notion page with all research

## Depth Levels

- quick: 5 min, 3-5 sources
- medium: 15 min, 8-10 sources (default)
- deep: 30 min, 15+ sources
