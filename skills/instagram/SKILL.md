# Instagram - Social Content Creation

Create engaging Instagram content ready to post.

## When to Use

Trigger on:
- "instagram post"
- "instagram carousel"
- "create carousel"
- "instagram content"
- "reels script"

## Workflow

1. **Carousel**: 8-10 slides with hook and CTA
2. **Caption**: Engaging text with hashtags
3. **Reels**: 3 short video scripts (15-30s)
4. **Visual notes**: Design concepts for each slide
5. **Save**: Notion page with all content

## Content Types

- carousel: Slide-by-slide content
- post: Single image posts
- reel: Video scripts
- all: Everything (default)
